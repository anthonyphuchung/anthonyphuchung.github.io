Hi 👋! I’m Anthony Chung, a junior at the University of Washington majoring in Computer Science with a strong passion for accessible technology, teaching, and boba. Feel free to check out some projects I have worked on!

An Investigation into Academic Performance Across Various Demographics
A data analysis project using data from data.wa.gov. In this project, my team and I examine the relationship socioeconomic status has with academic performance by looking into graduation rate, dropout rate, and student growth across demographics.
Campus Paths
A web application built with ReactJS applying Dijkstra's Algorithm to find the shortest path between two campus buildings at the University of Washington. The backend uses a custom-built Graph and a Spark server.
Mothering During Jim Crow: Using the Reproductive Justice Framework to Understand Black Motherhood During Legalized Racism
A qualitative analysis project using transcripts from Harvard's Black Women Oral History Project Interviews. In this project, my team and I investigate the systemic barriers of Jim Crow laws and their affect on the reproductive health of African-American women.
