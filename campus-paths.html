University of Washington's Campus Paths

Author: Anthony Chung

This project follows a model-view-controller design and is built using ReactJS. The web application uses Dijkstra's algorithm to draw the shortest path between two buildings on the University of Washington campus. The total distance and estimated walking time will be displayed at the bottom of the application.
