About Me

Hey there! My name is Anthony. I'm currently a junior at the University of Washington (UW) studying Computer Science with a minor in Education, Learning, and Society. I entered university intending to study aerospace engineering but my participation in Engineering Dean's Scholars, a summer college transition program, introduced me to the field of computer science. Through Engineering Dean's Scholars, I got the opportunity to tour Microsoft's campus in Redmond, WA. After seeing and interacting with Xbox's Adaptive Controller, I grew inspired by the positive change that technology was capable of and decided to pursue an education centered around creating accessible technology!

During the school year, I work as a teaching assistant for the Allen School and Engineering Academic Center by helping teach the introductory computer science series (CSE 14x) and running linear algebra workshops. Outside of school, I like to spend my spare time trying out new dishes, making Spotify playlists, and playing board/video games. If you need a food recommendation, try Xi'An Noodles!
